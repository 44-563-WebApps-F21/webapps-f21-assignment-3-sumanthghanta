Gorgon (real name Tomi Shishido) is a Marvel Comics villain and Mutant. who is frequently a foe to Wolverine and also a high-ranking member of Hydra and the Hand. True to his name, Gorgon is prone to turning innocent people into statues (particularly women), which he uses to decorate his palace.
